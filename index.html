About

I am a software engineer with expertise in cybersecurity, full-stack web development and embedded systems/IoT.
I obtained my Master's degree in Computer Science at Ca'Foscari University (Venice) and I have worked there for two years as a researcher. Additionally, I gained practical experience working at a university spin-off specialized in cyber-security and IoT.
Besides technology, I am passionate about music, holding a second Master's degree in Classical Guitar.
